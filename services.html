What I Do

Services

Structured where it matters. Practical where it counts. Built to last beyond my involvement.

01

Executive Performance & Accountability

Performance management is not an HR process — it's a governance tool. Boards and leadership teams need clear, structured approaches to executive evaluation that are grounded in documented expectations, not gut feel or annual ritual.

I design review frameworks, documentation standards, and accountability processes that work consistently — and hold up under scrutiny when it matters most.

  • Executive Director / CEO performance reviews
  • Performance improvement frameworks
  • Compensation and accountability alignment
  • Documentation and record-keeping standards
  • Goal-setting and expectation definition
  • Mid-cycle check-in structures
02

Strategic Goal Integration

Strategy documents are common. Strategy that actually reaches execution is not. The gap is usually a missing layer: defined milestones, clear ownership, and a cadence that makes progress visible before problems surface.

I build that layer — connecting strategic priorities to the operational structures that track them.

  • Milestone definition and sequencing
  • Progress tracking structures
  • Accountability ownership mapping
  • Reporting cadence design
  • Strategic alignment across functions
  • Quarterly and annual operating rhythms
03

Governance & Board Support

Effective boards need more than good intentions — they need working infrastructure. That means clear oversight structures, review processes that are consistent and documented, and governance systems that survive leadership transitions.

I work with boards and executive teams to build that infrastructure, particularly around executive accountability and continuity planning.

  • Executive oversight structure design
  • Board review process development
  • Governance documentation
  • Leadership transition planning
  • Nonprofit governance frameworks
  • Policy development support
04

Organizational Operating Systems

Growing organizations often outpace their internal structure. Decision-making becomes unclear. Cross-functional coordination depends on the right people being in the right room. Progress reports arrive too late to act on.

I build operating systems that distribute clarity — so leadership teams can move faster without losing accountability. This includes restructuring advisory when roles, teams, or priorities are shifting.

  • Leadership cadence design
  • Decision-making frameworks
  • Cross-functional alignment systems
  • Distributed team operating structures
  • Restructuring and transition advisory
  • Role clarity and accountability mapping
